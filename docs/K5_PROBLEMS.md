## Whole Numbers

### Writing Numbers in Standard Form

**Easy**
> Write the number {{word_form}} in standard form.
>
> *Example: Write the number "forty-two thousand, six hundred fifteen" in standard form.*

**Medium**
> {{name}} wrote a check for {{word_form_large}} dollars. Write this amount in standard form.
>
> *Example: Sarah wrote a check for "three hundred twenty-five thousand, four hundred eighteen" dollars. Write this amount in standard form.*

**Hard**
> A city has a population of {{word_form_millions}}. The neighboring city has {{word_form_millions_2}} people. Write both numbers in standard form and find the total population.
>
> *Example: A city has a population of "two million, four hundred fifty-six thousand, seven hundred twenty-three." The neighboring city has "one million, eight hundred ninety-two thousand, one hundred five" people.*

**Hard**

## Decimal Numbers

### Multiply Decimals by Whole Numbers

**Easy**

>

**Medium**

>

**Hard**

>
> *Example: Alex runs 2.75 kilometers every day. Ben runs 3.25 kilometers every day. After 12 days, how much farther has one runner gone than the other?*

### Multiply Decimals by Powers of 10

**Easy**
> Calculate {{decimal}} × {{power_of_10}}.
>
> *Example: Calculate 3.456 × 100.*

**Medium**
> A {{item}} weighs {{decimal}} {{small_unit}}. What is its weight in {{large_unit}}? (1 {{large_unit}} = {{power_of_10}} {{small_unit}})
>

**Hard**

>
> *Example: A factory produces 0.025 liters of juice per second. How much does it produce in 1000 seconds?*

### Dividing Decimals by Powers of 10

**Easy**
> Calculate {{decimal}} ÷ {{power_of_10}}.
>
> *Example: Calculate 45.6 ÷ 10.*

**Medium**
> {{name}} has ${{dollar_amount}}. If {{pronoun}} divides the money equally among {{power_of_10}} {{recipient_plural}}, how much does each {{recipient_singular}} get?
>
> *Example: Carlos has $250.00. If he divides the money equally among 100 students, how much does each student get?*

**Hard**

>
> *Example: A tank contains 5000.0 milliliters of medicine. It needs to be divided into 10 equal bottles, and each of those into 100 smaller vials. How much medicine is in each vial?*

### Convert Between Decimals and Fractions

**Medium**

>
> *Example: Find the mean of these numbers: 12, 18, 15, 21, 14.*
